Hello and welcome to the Rijksmeseum.

We are the Rijksmeseum adn we opened in 1885. The Rijksmeseum was created because wanted to spark love for the land again. Now 137 years later we are still going strong, keeping art save and also creating great expositions. The Rijksmeseum has changed a lot over the years and now finally we are online. Even now in these hard times we're working really hard to still be able to show people beatifull art and other things, and now we have an online Museum.
