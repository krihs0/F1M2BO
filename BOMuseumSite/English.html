Hello and welcome to the Rijksmeseum

We are the Rijksmeseum adn we opened in 1885. The Rijksmeseum was created because wanted to spark love for the land again.


Now 137 years later we are still going strong, keeping art save and also creating great expositions.


The Rijksmeseum has changed a lot over the years and now finally we are online. Even now in these hard times we're working really hard to still be able to show people beatifull art and other things, and now we have an online Museum.

Don't forget me


Portret of young lady

There isn't a lot of information about the revolutionary painting from the late fifteenth century. We do know who made the painting. It's Petrus Christus (1425-1476), a Vlaamse painter from the late gotiek. He can also be recognized by the weird way he paints, that indicates that he's from that time.
